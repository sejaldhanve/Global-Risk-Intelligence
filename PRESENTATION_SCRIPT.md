# Hackathon Presentation Script (Simple & Punchy)

**Goal**: Deliver a high-energy demo that judges can understand instantly.
**Roles**: 
- **Member 1 (The Hook)**: Grabs attention + Explains the "Why."
- **Member 2 (The Builder)**: Explains the "How" without getting lost in jargon.
- **Member 3 (The Pilot)**: Shows the "Wow" moments on screen.

---

### [0:00 - 0:45] The Problem (Member 1)
**Member 1**: "Imagine a war starts tomorrow. Within minutes, the internet is flooded with fake news. Prices of oil and food start jumping, but nobody knows why or by how much. 
Businesses are lost. People are panicked. 
We built **GRI (Global Risk Intelligence)** to solve this. It’s a 24/7 Command Center that cuts through the noise, finds the truth, and predicts the future."

---

### [0:45 - 1:45] The Tech (Member 2)
**Member 2**: "To build this, we didn't just use one source—we used them all. 
Our system automatically gathers news from Google, Bing, and major news outlets. 
But here’s the smart part: We use **GPT-4 as our Lead Analyst**. It reads everything, spots the lies, and finds the facts. 
Then, our **Custom AI Models** take those facts and calculate exactly how they will hit your wallet—predicting price changes for things like oil and gas before they even happen."

---

### [1:45 - 3:00] The Demo (Member 3 - Screen Sharing)
**Member 3**: "Let’s look at the map. (Point to screen) 
See these red zones? Those aren't just dots; they are real-time risk calculations. 
Let's search for 'Middle East Tensions.' 
(Wait for results) 
In seconds, GRI has analyzed dozens of articles. Look at the **Forecasting Block**—it’s predicting a 10% rise in gas prices. 
And if you have a specific question, just ask our **AI Assistant**. (Type: 'Is the Suez Canal safe?') 
It’s not just giving a generic answer; it’s looking at the data it just found to give you a real, actionable report."

---

### [3:00 - 3:45] The Big Finish (Member 1)
**Member 1**: "GRI turns 'Information Overload' into 'Intelligence.' We’re helping the world stay one step ahead of chaos. 
Whether you're a business owner or a humanitarian, GRI gives you the clarity to act fast. 
We are Global Risk Intelligence. Thank you!"

---

### 💡 Tips for a Winning Demo:
- **Don't Read**: Use these bullet points as a guide, but keep your eyes on the judges.
- **Show, Don't Just Tell**: Make sure Member 3 is clicking through the map while Member 2 is explaining the tech.
- **Energy is Key**: If you sound excited about your project, the judges will be too!
