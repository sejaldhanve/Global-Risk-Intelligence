# Hackathon Q&A: The Simple Guide

Keep your answers short, confident, and focused on the "Value."

## 🛠️ The Tech Stuff (Simple Answers)

**Judge: "How do you know what news is real or fake?"**
*   **Your Answer**: "We use 'AI Consensus.' Our system reads many different news sources at once. If only one source says something, it’s a warning. If five sources agree, it’s a fact. We let the AI find the overlap to cut out the bias."

**Judge: "Why use both Node.js and Python?"**
*   **Your Answer**: "Node.js is the 'Speed Daemon'—it makes the website super fast and responsive. Python is the 'Mathematician'—it’s the best at doing the complex risk calculations and price predictions. By using both, we get the best of both worlds."

**Judge: "What makes your AI 'Agentic'?"**
*   **Your Answer**: "Most AI just talks. Our AI *acts*. It doesn't just answer your question; it actually triggers our search tools and prediction models to find the data itself. It’s like having a researcher who also writes the report."

## 🌍 The Business Stuff (Simple Answers)

**Judge: "Who would actually pay for this?"**
*   **Your Answer**: "Anyone who loses money when the world changes. Shipping companies, energy investors, and even humanitarian groups who need to know where it's safe to send help. We turn news into a 'Risk Score' they can use."

**Judge: "What’s your advantage over a regular news site?"**
*   **Your Answer**: "A news site tells you *what* happened. GRI tells you *what happens next*. We don't just give you a headline; we give you a price prediction and a risk map."

**Judge: "What’s the biggest challenge you faced?"**
*   **Your Answer**: "Connecting everything! Taking raw, messy news from the internet and turning it into a clean, mathematical price forecast in under 10 seconds was our biggest technical win."

## 🚀 Final Advice for the Q&A:
- **Be Honest**: If you don't know an answer, say: *"That's a great point for our version 2.0. Right now, we're focused on [X], but we plan to add [Y] next."*
- **Teamwork**: If a question is about tech, let the Engineer answer. If it's about the vision, let the Visionary answer. 
- **Confidence**: You built this! You are the experts on this project.
